The Credentials

A family of professional credentials.

Each CPA credential is built from the same modular core, then specialised to a function. All carry CPD hours and are gated to certified senior practitioners and AI-supported aftercare.

CPAE

Certified Professional Agile Executive

The Certified Professional Agile Executive course teaches participants everything they need to lead Agile organisations. By the end of the course participants will have attained all of the knowledge and skills to support an Agile organisation and deliver value faster.

2 days closed cohort CPD-bearing

CPAPO

Certified Professional Agile Product Owner

The Certified Professional Agile Product Owner course teaches the basics of Product Ownership. By the end of the course participants will have attained all of the knowledge and skills to successfully perform the role of Product Owner.

3 days open & closed cohorts CPD-bearing
Find a class
CPASM

Certified Professional Agile Scrum Master

The Certified Professional Agile Scrum Master certification proves you have attained enough knowledge to perform the servant leader role as a Scrum Master. By the end of the course you will be ready to start life as a guardian of performance.

3 days open & closed cohorts CPD-bearing

CPAT

Certified Professional Agile Team Member

The Certified Professional Agile Team Member course gives delivery team members everything they need to operate effectively inside an Agile team. By the end of the course participants will have attained the knowledge and skills to contribute, collaborate, and deliver value as a high-performing team member.

2 days open & closed cohorts CPD-bearing

CPAF

Certified Professional Agile Foundations

The Certified Professional Agile Foundations course provides you with an understanding of the Agile mindset, key roles, events and artefacts and enables you to understand and experience how to participate on Professional Agile teams.

2 days open cohorts CPD-bearing

CPAFK

Certified Professional Agile Foundations with Kanban

The Certified Professional Agile Foundations with Kanban course gives you a one-day introduction to the Agile mindset and the practical disciplines of Kanban. By the end of the day you will understand how to visualise work, manage flow, and contribute to a team practising continuous delivery.

1 day open cohorts CPD-bearing
