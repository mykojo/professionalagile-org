Governance

How the standard is held.

A standards body is only as good as the discipline that maintains it. Three governance lines hold the CPA framework in trust.

01

Curriculum stewardship

A standing curriculum board reviews the modular library annually. Modules are versioned, retired, or augmented based on practitioner feedback and market evidence. The board's decisions are documented and published in the standards version log.

02

Practitioner accreditation

Senior practitioners are accredited through a defined assessment pipeline before being permitted to deliver any CPA credential. No cohort is taught by an unaccredited practitioner. Accreditation is renewed annually.

03

Partner accreditation

Delivery partners are accredited per credential and per market. Exclusivity, where granted, is documented and time-bound. The current lead delivery partner is Agile Capability LLC.
