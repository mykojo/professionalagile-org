The framework

What is CPA.

Read the full framework

The Certified Professional Agile framework is a modular, accredited body of knowledge. A standard. A set of credentials. And a delivery discipline.

It exists because the dominant agile frameworks were authored for software development teams, then retro-fitted onto everything else. CPA was authored from the opposite direction. We started with the professional function, with the operating reality of the African enterprise it serves, and only then brought in agile principles where they earned their place.

The framework holds because it was tested against the real working conditions of African banks, insurers, infrastructure integrators and incubators. From there it travelled. Today the framework is in delivery to organisations on three continents.
