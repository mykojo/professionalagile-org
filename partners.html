How the network works

Partnership principles.

Accreditation

Every partner on the network is accredited to deliver against the CPA framework. Practitioners are individually assessed. The standard does not bend.

Exclusivity, where granted

Some credentials and some markets are licensed to single partners. Where exclusivity exists, it is documented and time-bound. The current exclusive partner for CPAHR in selected markets is Agile Capability LLC.

Selection

The partner network expands selectively, by invitation and by application. We are not a franchise. We are a standard.
